About us

Greecania is inspired by the age-old traditional ways, based on unique and original recipes combined from the genuine Greek origin. Expertise and know how all the way from Greece. Custom state-of-the-art, unique and trade protected machinery to gives us an absolute competitive edge.

A variety of tastes & flavors is what we offer. A plethora of aromatic food items. Pungent tastes laced with delicate seasoning taking the taste of the dish to another level. You are sure to get lost in the indulgence of sauces & dips. We also produce fruit spreads and preserves. Enriched with fresh fruits, these spreads in combination with other foods are sure to make your day. Our Yogurts, ice-creams & puddings are a cut above the rest. A number of flavors to choose from where choice becomes difficult. Fresh packaged drinking water to keep yourself hydrated

VISION

We want to introduce the heritage of greek food culture to Indian consumers and beyond.

MISSION

Building an eco system where the consumption of milk is not limited to curd and butter but leveraging the perporties of the segment to derive new possibilities everyday.
